Games are for Everyone!

Welcome to my games web page. I personally love games of all sorts, video games, board games, card games, but generally not head games. On this page I will be cataloguing and describing some of the games I have at home. You will also be able to add some of your favorite games.

Here are some of the categories for my board games

  • Family games These are games that are good for all ages. Some of my favorites include Connect 4, Pickomino, and parchisi.
  • Logic Games This is my favorite board game category. This could actually be broken up into several sub-categories as well. We have friends that come over for a monthly game night, and we usually play an epic game like Terraforming Mars, Puerto Rico, or Domaine. These are games that tend to last 2-3 hours, sometimes even longer. Other logic games that are more in keeping with the typical 30-45 minute family game include domination and blockus.
  • Party Games These are games that are more fun the more people you have. Usually, while there is still a winner, winning isn't the goal so much as laughing is. When I have a group of people over who have different intersts and gaming backgrounds, I like to play Monikers, Cards Against Humanity, Poetry for Neanderthals or Taboo.
  • Other Games Some games either fit multiple categories or don't quite fit into the categories listed above, so they are in the "other" category. I included all of my two-player-only games, such as pentago, backgammon, and hive as well as some hard-to classify card games like exploding kittens and Here to Slay.
